Everyone warned Croc about the pink house, the only house off limits...

But Croc was too curious, he even went by Curious Croc like Curious George

He had to know more. Why was it off limits? And why was it pink?

"Curious Croc is afraid of nothing!"

Croc would pass by the house almost every night,

wondering if he should enter...

Waiting for something to happen that would draw him in

And, then, one fateful night...

"Oh my dog what is that thing in the door?"

He went closer, the curiosity was killing him...

There was something different about the house tonight, but what?

There's something similar in the window too,
what is going on?!

Croc was too curious, he could no longer withstand the light from within...


Maybe this will tell me why the house is pink...
Here I gooo...
Remember me forever as Curious Croc

Croc was never seen again.

The pink house remained as spooky as it ever was.
